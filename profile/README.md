# 🛡️ Zbelthas: The Zero-Trust Digital Ecosystem

Zbelthas is a complete digital security platform with three integrated modules: **Wallet, Chat, and Browser**. It runs entirely on your device, protecting your digital life with military-grade cryptography.

---

## 🚀 What Makes Zbelthas Different

*   **Non-Custodial:** We don't hold your keys. You maintain complete control.
*   **Post-Quantum Ready:** Protection against future quantum computing threats.
*   **Zero Data Collection:** No databases to breach, no logs to subpoena.
*   **Hardware Security:** Integration with TPM, Secure Enclave, and StrongBox.
